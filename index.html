Welcome to Estanbouli, Hillman, Purdham and Serendip

We are a Ontario-based consultancy firm offering Geographic Information Systems solutions to municipalities and private industry.

As data asset control and analysis becomes increasingly critical for strategic planning, process optimization and risk management, so too does the need increase to link that control and analysis to concrete spatial and temporal schemas to uncover patterns and aid predictive modelling.

Our team of GIS professionals support a variety of industries in aligning best-practice solutions with client needs.

With special emphasis on communications and educating our clients on options and potentials of this rapidly evolving technology, EHPS is your go-to resource for discovering how GIS will reveal options and resources previously unseen.
